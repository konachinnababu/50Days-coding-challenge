##  Project Overview

This project analyzes how eating out habits affect health indicators such as BMI, cholesterol levels, blood pressure, and overall health score. The dashboard provides insights into lifestyle patterns and their impact on health.

---

##  Objectives

* Analyze the relationship between eating out frequency and health
* Identify high-risk individuals based on BMI and cholesterol
* Understand how lifestyle factors (exercise, sleep, calories) influence health
* Build an interactive Power BI dashboard

---

##  Dataset Details

The dataset contains 200 records with the following fields:

* Age, Gender, Occupation
* Eat_Out_Frequency (per week)
* Preferred_EatOut_Type (FastFood, CasualDining, StreetFood, HealthyCafe)
* Daily_Calories
* Exercise_Hours_Per_Week
* Sleep_Hours
* BMI
* BloodPressure
* Cholesterol_Level
* Diabetes
* Health_Score

---

##  Dashboard Features

###  KPI Metrics

* Average Eat-Out Frequency
* Average Health Score
* % of People with Diabetes
* % of People with Hypertension

---

### Visualizations

* Bar Chart: Eat-Out Frequency vs Average BMI
* Line Chart: Eat-Out Frequency vs Health Score
* Scatter Plot: Daily Calories vs Health Score
* Stacked Column Chart: Eat-Out Type vs Cholesterol Levels
* Pie Chart: Distribution of Eating Out Types

---

###  Advanced Features

* DAX Measures (Avg BMI, High Risk Count, Eat-Out Impact)
* Conditional Formatting (Highlight low health scores & high cholesterol)
* Interactive Slicers (Gender, Age Group, Occupation)

---

##  Key Insights

* Higher eating out frequency leads to increased BMI and lower health scores
* Fast food consumption is associated with higher cholesterol levels
* Regular exercise helps reduce negative health impacts
* Young professionals and students tend to eat out more frequently

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates how lifestyle habits, especially eating out, significantly impact health. It also highlights the importance of exercise and balanced diet in maintaining good health.
